# Requirement Defect Classification

## Requirement 1
System should be fast

Defect Type:
- Ambiguity
- Non-verifiable

Reason:
The word "fast" is unclear and not measurable.

---

## Requirement 2
User login must be secure and quick

Defect Type:
- Ambiguity
- Non-verifiable

Reason:
The terms "secure" and "quick" are not clearly defined and cannot be tested.

---

## Requirement 3
System shall respond in less than 2 seconds

Defect Type:
- None

Reason:
This requirement is clear, measurable, and testable.
